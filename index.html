Why Choose School of Open Learning?

About SOL

The School of Open Learning (SOL), affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), was established in 2018 to provide inclusive, affordable, and flexible education. As a pioneering institution in School Open and Distance Learning (SODL) in India, SOL is committed to empowering learners from all walks of life.

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education through open and distance learning to diverse sections of society, especially those who are unable to pursue regular full-time courses.

Key Advantages
  • Recognized by UGC and DEB
  • Study materials in both English and Marathi
  • Personal Contact Programs (PCPs) at study centers
  • Affordable fee structure
  • Valued degree equivalent to regular programs

Examination System

Examination Process
Key Features
  • Twice-yearly examination cycles (June and December)
  • Semester pattern for PG programs, annual pattern for UG programs
  • Backlog examination facility
  • Revaluation and photocopy of answer scripts available
Important Notes
  • Minimum 75% attendance in PCPs required for exam eligibility
  • Exam form submission deadlines strictly enforced
  • Hall tickets available online 15 days before exams
  • Results typically declared within 45 days of exams
